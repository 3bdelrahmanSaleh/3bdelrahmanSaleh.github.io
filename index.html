Who I Am

Abdelrahman Saleh — GIS Specialist

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Geography (GIS Division) from Menoufia University, complemented by professional certifications in GIS, spatial analysis, and remote sensing from Esri Academy and specialized remote sensing training from Hexagon.

For my graduation project, I conducted a comprehensive field survey of Wadi El-Shallal, Matrouh Governorate — deploying Total Station, GPS, and Level instruments and integrating field data with GIS for full spatial analysis and terrain modelling.

I am driven by geospatial technologies and their power to address environmental and spatial challenges, consistently advancing my skills across GIS, remote sensing, and cartographic visualization.

My GIS Work

Sand Dune Movement Analysis using ArcGIS
Monitored sand dune migration patterns using multi-temporal satellite imagery in ArcGIS Pro, applying change detection to quantify dune displacement rates over five years. Delivered movement maps and reports to environmental management authorities for desertification mitigation.
ArcGIS ProRemote SensingChange Detection
Solid Waste Landfill Suitability Analysis
Developed a multi-criteria GIS model integrating geology, land use, population density, and transport networks to rank candidate landfill sites via weighted overlay analysis. Results provided decision-makers a scientifically validated shortlist, reducing risk.
ArcGIS ProMulti-Criteria AnalysisWeighted Overlay
Wadi El-Shallal Field Survey & GIS Mapping
Conducted a comprehensive field survey of Wadi El-Shallal, Matrouh, using Total Station, GPS, and Level instruments. Integrated all measurements into GIS for spatial analysis, contour mapping, and terrain modelling, producing a full topographic map.
Total StationGPSArcGIS
Land Use / Land Cover Change Detection
Classified and compared land cover maps from two time periods using Landsat imagery and supervised classification in ArcGIS and ERDAS Imagine. Findings contributed to a regional environmental assessment on urban expansion impacts.
LandsatERDAS ImagineClassification
Vegetation Health Monitoring with NDVI
Computed NDVI time series from Sentinel-2 imagery to assess seasonal vegetation health, correlating anomalies with drought and soil degradation using Google Earth Engine. Produced monthly health maps for agricultural extension offices.
Sentinel-2Google Earth EngineNDVI
GIS for Climate Action — Spatial Assessment
Applied spatial analysis to assess climate vulnerabilities including flood risk zones, heat islands, and land degradation hotspots using multi-source datasets in ArcGIS Pro. Outputs formatted as policy-ready spatial reports aligned with SDGs.
ArcGIS ProClimate RiskSDGs
